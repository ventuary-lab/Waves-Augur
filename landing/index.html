Welcome to the DAO!
Please install Waves Keeper first

It is a browser extension that allows to create your Waves wallet and manage your private keys to interact with our platform

Available in Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Opera and Brave

Install Waves Keeper
  • Watch video instructions
  • Ask for help in Telegram group
Create a Waves wallet
Click “Add account” in your Waves Keeper browser extension and select “Create a new account”, or click “Import Account” below  if you already have a Waves wallet.

Don’t forget to make sure that the extension is in "Mainnet" mode!

If you’ve done this, click on “Got it” to proceed!
  • Watch video instructions
  • Ask for help in Telegram group
Check Waves Keeper permissions
If you haven’t yet given Waves Keeper access to Ventuary DAO, you will need to do the following steps:
  1. Open Waves Keeper.
  2. Go to Settings menu (in the right upper corner)
  3. Open Permissions Control menu
  4. Give access to beta.ventuary.space by clicking a power button icon on the left
  • Watch video instructions
  • Ask for help in Telegram group
Finally, request an invite

You require an invitation link to join us. There are two ways to get it:

Ask for invitation in our Telegram group
or
Fill in the invitation request here

When you receive your personal invite link, you can use it only once to register on Ventuary DAO.

Last but not least: before you click on your invite link to register, make sure that your active Waves Keeper address is on mainnet and that its balance is equal to at least 0.01 WAVES — you will need to spend a 0.009 fee to register your profile on the blockchain
